数据集 / Datasets

数据集名称 / Dataset Name: 固定点碰摩转子实验数据 / Fixed-point Rubbing Rotor Experimental Data

数据简介 / Description: 该数据集来源于马辉教授发表于《Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing》的论文。实验采用碰摩实验平台，研究固定点碰摩下的转子动力学响应。平台包括双盘转子、可调限位装置及传感器系统，采集频率为2000 Hz。通过改变转速与碰摩间隙模拟不同工况。
This dataset is derived from Prof. Ma Hui’s publication in Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing. A rubbing test rig is used to investigate the dynamic behavior of a rotor system under fixed-point rubbing conditions. The rig consists of a dual-disc rotor, adjustable limiters, and a sensor system. The sampling frequency is 2000 Hz, and varying speeds and gap distances are used to simulate different working conditions.
